The Last Fighter

High Concept

Asteroids meets Galaga


Genre

Shoot-em-up and around. Asteroids style.

Action, Shooter, Fighting, Hunting.


Platform

Desktop only


Story

As the last fighter of an invaded planet, you know your time is up. As a last stand, a testmant to your planet, you give the intergalactic forces a fight to the death, taking down swarms of enemies.


Aesthetics

Futuristic sci-fi space.


Gameplay

Mechanics

Enemies spawn in from the screen, chasing after you, shooting at you, trying to destroy you!

Destroy all enemies using your blasters!

Survive for as long as possible with the highest score possible.

Every 10 enemies killed, you can upgrade your arsenal, gain more health, or increase your speed!

Controls

  • WASD keys to move the ship
  • Mouse to aim the bullets
  • Spacebar to shoot bullets
  • Shift to accelerate speed!

Teaching the game

There is a simple tutorial page that the player will click on to see what the controls are.

Player strategy

Players will quickly learn to make use of the accelerate button and also to pickup powerups to make themselves more powerful and to gain more health.
